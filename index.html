Blood Donation

Why Donate Blood?

Donating blood is a simple, safe way to save lives.

Every donation you make can save up to three lives. Blood is needed every two seconds, and there’s no substitute for it.

Donate Now

How It Works

1. Register

Sign up and fill out your details. We'll guide you through the simple process of becoming a donor.

2. Donate

Visit our center or participate in our mobile blood drives. The donation process only takes about 10 minutes.

3. Save Lives

Your blood can help those in need, from accident victims to cancer patients. Every drop counts.
